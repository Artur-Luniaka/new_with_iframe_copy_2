Dojo Rules

Welcome to Ninja Rabbit! By stepping into our dojo, you agree to follow these simple rules to maintain a fun and fair environment for all warriors.

  • Honorable Combat: Use only legitimate combat techniques. Any form of cheating or exploitation will result in account penalties.
  • Scroll Security: Keep your account information secure and never share your credentials with others.
  • Respectful Conduct: Be respectful to other ninjas. Harassment or inappropriate behavior is not tolerated.

Warrior Safety Guidelines

Your safety and comfort while training are our top priorities. Please follow these guidelines for the best gaming experience:

  • Training Breaks: Take regular breaks, especially during long training sessions.
  • Environment: Train in a well-lit area and maintain proper posture.
  • Age Restrictions: This game is suitable for players aged 7 and above.
