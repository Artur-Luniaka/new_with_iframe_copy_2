Cookie Policy

Understanding Our Scroll Usage

Learn how we use digital scrolls (cookies) to enhance your ninja adventure and protect your privacy.

What Are Digital Scrolls (Cookies)?

Digital scrolls are small text files placed on your device when you visit our training grounds. They help us provide you with a better gaming experience by remembering your preferences and ninja progress.

  • Temporary Scrolls: Expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Scrolls: Remain on your device for a set period.
  • First-Party Scrolls: Scrolls set by our website directly.

How We Use Digital Scrolls

We use digital scrolls for various purposes to improve your ninja experience:

  • Essential Scrolls: Required for basic game functionality and security.
  • Preference Scrolls: Remember your game settings and customizations.
  • Dojo Analytics Scrolls: Help us understand how warriors interact with our adventure.

Your Scroll Choices

You have control over how digital scrolls are used on your device:

  • You can modify your browser settings to accept or reject scrolls.
  • Disabling essential scrolls may affect game functionality.
  • You can delete scrolls at any time through your browser settings.

Types of Data We Collect

Through digital scrolls, we may collect the following information:

  • Warrior Progress: Your levels, scores, and achievements in Ninja Rabbit
  • Preferences: Your game settings and customizations
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and session data

Updates to This Policy

Please note the following about our digital scroll policy:

  • We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices
  • Continued use of our ninja adventure implies acceptance of our cookie policy
  • Contact us if you have questions about our scroll usage
